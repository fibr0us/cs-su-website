Active Research Highlights

A closer look at the groundbreaking research happening across our four research groups.

ESBMC Model Checker

Prof Bernd Fischer leads development of the Efficient SMT-Based Context-Bounded Model Checker, used worldwide for verifying C/C++ and Solidity programs. Latest version supports verification of smart contracts and multi-threaded code.

Swarm Intelligence & Neural Nets

Prof Andries Engelbrecht's group advances particle swarm optimisation, differential evolution, and deep learning architectures — with applications in financial modelling, scheduling, and bioinformatics.

Assistive Technology for Deaf Learners

Prof Lynette van Zijl's team develops accessible computing tools and South African Sign Language resources, working directly with the deaf community and schools to bridge the digital divide.

COASTAL Concolic Analyser

Prof Willem Visser's COASTAL framework combines concrete and symbolic execution to automatically find bugs in Java programs. The tool is open source and actively used in software verification research.
