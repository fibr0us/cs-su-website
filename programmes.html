Application & Deadlines

Stay ahead of key dates for the upcoming academic year. Mark your calendar and ensure your application is complete on time.

Online Application Portal Opens

All undergraduate and postgraduate applications are submitted via the Stellenbosch University online portal. Prepare your academic transcripts, motivational letter, and supporting documents.

Apply at sun.ac.za
2 February 2026

Honours Orientation Day

Compulsory orientation session for all incoming BCmp Honours students. Covers programme structure, research project expectations, and departmental resources.

6 February 2026

Honours Project Proposals Due

Submission deadline for Honours research project proposals. Students must submit their proposed research question, methodology, and supervisor agreement by this date.

9 February 2026

Honours Lectures Begin

Formal coursework lectures commence for all BCmp Honours students. Attendance is compulsory. Module schedules will be distributed during orientation.

Postgraduate Rolling Admissions

Master's and PhD applications are considered on a rolling basis subject to supervisor availability. Contact the postgraduate coordinator early to discuss potential research topics.
